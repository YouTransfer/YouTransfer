Finalise

If you've completed configuring your YouTransfer instance, it is recommended to finalise the settings. This will remove the 'Settings' link from the bottom-right corner. Access to the Settings pages are restricted.

Once the settings have been finalised, changes must be done directly in the 'settings.json' file located in the root of the installation directory. You can also unlock the settings, which will require you to provide an unlock code.
